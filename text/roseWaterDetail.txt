Rose water is a fragrant liquid made from steeping rose petals in water. It has a light floral scent and is used in a variety of products, including:

Skincare: Rose water is a common ingredient in beauty products due to its anti-inflammatory properties, which can help soothe and hydrate the skin.

Cooking: Rose water is used as a flavoring in various cuisines, particularly in Middle Eastern and Indian sweets.

Aromatherapy: Rose water is used as a natural air freshener and is believed to have a calming effect on the mind and body.

Perfumery: Rose water is used as a base for making perfumes and as a fixative to help other fragrances last longer.
