Gulkand is a sweet preserve made from rose petals and sugar. It is used in cuisine and Ayurvedic medicine.<br>In Ayurveda, gulkand is believed to have several health benefits, including improving digestion, reducing stress and anxiety, and supporting overall heart health. It is often consumed as a spread or added to desserts and drinks. The process of making gulkand involves slow cooking rose petals with sugar and other natural ingredients over a period of several days until the mixture forms a thick, sweet paste.
