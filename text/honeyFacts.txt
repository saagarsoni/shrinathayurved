One tablespoon (21 grams) of honey contains approximately 64 calories and 17 grams of sugar.<br>
Honey is a source of carbohydrates and contains small amounts of vitamins and minerals, including vitamin B6, niacin, riboflavin, pantothenic acid, calcium, iron, and zinc.<br>
Honey is also a source of antioxidants, which are compounds that can help protect the body against damage from free radicals.<br>
It's important to note that, as a sweetener, honey should be consumed in moderation as part of a balanced diet.<br>
Additionally, because it is high in fructose, it should be used with caution by individuals with fructose intolerance or other conditions that affect fructose metabolism.
