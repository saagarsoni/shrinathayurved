1. **Lemon Juice or Concentrate:** The primary source of flavor in lemon squash comes from lemon juice or concentrated lemon juice.

2. **Sugar or Sweeteners:** Sugar is often added to balance the tartness of the lemon and provide sweetness. Some variations may use alternative sweeteners.

3. **Water:** To dilute the lemon juice and achieve the desired consistency.

4. **Citric Acid:** Like in orange squash, citric acid may be added to enhance the tartness and act as a natural preservative.

5. **Preservatives:** Some lemon squash products may include preservatives to prolong shelf life.

6. **Flavor Enhancers:** Natural or artificial flavor enhancers might be added to intensify the lemon flavor.

7. **Coloring Agents:** Some brands may use natural or artificial coloring agents to give the lemon squash an appealing color.

8. **Vitamins and Minerals:** Certain brands may add additional vitamins and minerals for nutritional value.
